‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download the Routee app, tap "Sign Up", enter your email and create a password. You'll receive a verification email to activate your account.

How do I add money to my wallet?

Go to the Wallet section in the app, tap "Buy Points", choose your preferred payment method, and follow the secure checkout process.

How do I register my vehicle?

Navigate to "My Vehicles" in the dashboard, tap "Add Vehicle", and fill in your vehicle details including registration, insurance, and photos.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. We never share your data with third parties without your consent.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact us via email at brhimakil.1@gmail.com or through WhatsApp at +961 70 049 615. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
